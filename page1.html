Mission Statement:
Young People are not educated with their finances with the current school system. With this website young people can carry out good banking practices and build healthy money habits early on in their lives and continue their life with smart financial decisions.
    Goals:
  • Assist in student loans planning
  • Educate in basic savings/checking accounts
  • Educate in alternative money placement options, i.e. mutual funds, stock market
  • Inform about Credit
  • Assist in saving for trips
